## 3. Physical Interpretation of Regime Dynamics

### The CASES-99 "Hockey-Stick Transition" (HOST)

Its high entropy reflects the clean, uncollapsed transition from the convective daytime mixer to the stably stratified nocturnal boundary layer. It serves as the ultimate test for Single-Column Models (SCMs) because it proves whether an operational weather model's parametrization can transition smoothly through state space without artificial numerical truncation or premature "runaway cooling" (which causes premature attractor collapse).
